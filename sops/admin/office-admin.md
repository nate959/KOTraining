# New Client Intake & Scheduling

This procedure details how the office admin handles a newly incoming lead.

## Step 1: Lead Capture
- Answer phone with: "Thank you for calling Knockout Inspections, how can we help you?"
- Collect Name, Phone, Email, and Property Address.

## Step 2: Quoting
Open the Knockout Master Pricing App:
- Input the square footage and age of the home.
- Ask if they need ancillary services (Wind Mitigation, 4-Point, WDO/Termite, or FORTIFIED Evaluation).
- Generate the quote.

## Step 3: ISN Entry
- Open ISN (Inspection Support Network).
- Create a new order.
- Select the assigned inspector based on territory logic.
- Send the automated scheduling email and agreement link.

## Step 4: Follow Up
- If the agreement remains unsigned 24 hours prior to inspection, call the client to verify they intend to proceed.
