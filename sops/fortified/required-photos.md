# Required Photos for FORTIFIED Evaluations

Every FORTIFIED evaluation requires a strict set of photos to be submitted to IBHS. This SOP outlines the absolute minimum photos required before leaving a site.

## General Requirements
1. **Address Verification:** Must clearly show the mailbox or house numbers.
2. **Elevations:** All 4 sides of the home must be documented.

## Roof Deck & Covering
3. **Deck Attachment:** (If applicable) Must clearly show ring shank nails.
4. **Underlayment:** Showing a sealed roof deck, specifically tape over seams or a fully adhered membrane.
5. **Drip Edge Installation:** Must show drip edge installed *over* underlayment at the eaves, and *under* underlayment at the rakes.
6. **Roof Cover Attachment:** Shingles showing 6 nails per shingle for High Wind zones.

> [!WARNING]
> Missing any of these photos will result in a delay or denial of the FORTIFIED certification. Always use the Knockout Inspection App to ensure no photos are missed before leaving the property.
