# FORTIFIED Evaluation Guidelines

How to conduct a proper FORTIFIED Evaluation for IBHS certification.

## Required Photos (Minimum)
Every evaluation requires a strict set of photos to be submitted to IBHS.

1. **Address verification** (Mailbox or house numbers)
2. **Elevations** (All 4 sides of the home)
3. **Deck attachment** (If applicable, showing ring shank nails)
4. **Underlayment** (Sealed roof deck showing tape over seams or fully adhered membrane)
5. **Drip edge installation** (Over underlayment at eaves, under at rakes)
6. **Roof cover attachment** (Shingles showing 6 nails per shingle for High Wind zones)

> Note: Missing any of these photos will result in a delay or denial of the FORTIFIED certification. Use the Knockout Inspection App to ensure no photos are missed.

## Common Fail Points
- Nails too far apart on the roof deck (must be max 6 inches on center).
- Wrong type of sealant used on the seams.

Refer to the IBHS official manual for specific regional code requirements.
