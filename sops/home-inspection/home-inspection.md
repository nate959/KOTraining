# Standard Home Inspection Process

This document outlines the standard operating procedure for performing a residential home inspection for Knockout Inspections.

## 1. Pre-Inspection Prep
- Review the client details in ISN.
- Ensure drone batteries are charged.
- Confirm tools are in the truck (Ladder, Flashlight, Moisture Meter, Outlet Tester).

## 2. On-Site Client Greeting
- Arrive 15 minutes early.
- Greet client / agent professionally. Give them the Knockout standard pitch.
- Explain the flow of the inspection (Outside -> Roof -> Attic -> Inside -> Wrap up).

## 3. Exterior Inspection
- Start at the front door and work clockwise around the property.
- Document grading, siding, windows, and doors.
- Take 4 elevation photos (Front, Right, Rear, Left).

## 4. Roof Inspection
- If safe, walk the roof. Otherwise, use the DJI Drone.
- Inspect all penetrations, flashings, valleys, and ridges.
- Identify roof age and potential wind mitigation features.

## 5. Summary & Handout
- Give the client a quick verbal rundown of major defects.
- Remind them they will receive the full report within 24 hours.
- Mention we can also add a FORTIFIED Evaluation if they qualify.
